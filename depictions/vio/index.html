Violet

Show the artwork of your current playing song on more areas than just the music app itself

Features:

    Music

  • - artwork as background
  • - light, dark or adaptive blur
  • - artwork opacity
  • - artwork dim
  • - hide grabber
  • - hide artwork
  • - hide song title
  • - hide artist button
  • - hide more button
  • - hide time slider
  • - hide time slider knob
  • - hide elapsed time label
  • - hide remaining time label
  • - hide volume slider
  • - hide minimum volume image
  • - hide maximum volume image
  • - hide lyrics button
  • - hide airplay button
  • - hide airplay label
  • - hide queue button

    • Spotify

  • - artwork as background
  • - light, dark or adaptive blur
  • - artwork opacity
  • - artwork dim
  • - hide artwork
  • - hide next track button
  • - hide previous track button
  • - hide pause/play button
  • - hide shuffle button
  • - hide repeat button
  • - hide devices button
  • - hide queue button
  • - hide song title, artist name, playlist title
  • - hide time slider
  • - hide remaining time label
  • - hide elapsed time label
  • - hide like button
  • - hide back button
  • - hide context
  • - hide playlist title
  • - hide canvas
  • - use rounded mini player corners

    • Lockscreen

  • - artwork as background
  • - light, dark or adaptive blur
  • - artwork opacity
  • - artwork dim
  • - artwork transition
  • - artwork as player background
  • - light or dark player blur
  • - player artwork opacity
  • - player artwork corner radius
  • - artwork transition
  • - hide player background
  • - roundlockscreen compatibility
  • - hide xen html widgets while playing

    • Homescreen

  • - artwork as background
  • - light, dark or adaptive blur
  • - artwork opacity
  • - artwork dim
  • - artwork transition
  • - zoomed view

    • Control Center

  • - artwork as background
  • - light, dark or adaptive blur
  • - artwork opacity
  • - artwork dim
  • - artwork transition
  • - artwork as module background
  • - module light, dark or adaptive blur
  • - module artwork corner radius
  • - module artwork opacity
  • - module artwork dim
  • - artwork transition

    • Other Options

  • - respring button
  • - reset preferences button
